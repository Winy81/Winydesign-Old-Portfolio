What you should know:


The email templates designed, has been made with pure HTML.

What you can see on the site, is the layout that the recipient will see upon recieving you're email.

You can open the source code with the button under the templates.

Just copy out, change the text to suit your needs and then impliment to your email account.

Multiple clients can decode the source code differently so that further layout options can be made possible...
